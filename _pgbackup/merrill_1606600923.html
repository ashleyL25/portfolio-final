This Project was an internship I completed for Merrill Manufacturing in Storm Lake, IA during 2018. My main task was to build an ecommerce website on Shopify. Alongside my main task, I completed small tasks throughout the year.


Website Graphics

Graphics elements used throughout the Merrill Manufacturing website.


1/4

Bury Depth Map made into an interactive tooltip for the information tab on the website. Made in Adobe Illustrator to represent the bury depth messurements accross the United States. Converted to be responsive on website so when the consumer hovers over a colored area, it triggered a popup containing more information about their areas bury depth. Applied below every product page.

2/4

Designed for the product page. Used as a hero image on the top of the page to help guide the user to the product they need.

3/4

Used on the "About Us" Page. Old photos were scanned and then were worked on in Photoshop to increase their quality so they were clear and sharp on the website.

4/4

Used as a banner on the "About Us" page. Revived an old photo taken in 1948 to make it digitally customary. Used this graphic to display the companies mission statement with a ripped-paper layed on top to give depth to the photo.


Facebook Ad Champaign

A Facebook adverising campaign to portray the four values of Merrill Manufacturing to their social media audience. Contains a string of 4 designs, each with a value, description and graphic to portay the value. The main theme of the advertising campaign was water and hometown.
